Universal Selling Point
  • 1. Hard, Compact, Light-weight unit with Environment friendly IP67 or IP65 plastic casing.
  • 2. Smart data acquisition – Optimized based on Event, Time, Angle, Ignition & Distance.
  • 3. GPS Dark zone / multi path elimination using LBS & Cell Tower Triangulation.
  • 4. Virtual trip meter with automatic Trip start and Trip end detection.
  • 5. Power / SIM / Battery & Box Tamper detection & Emergency (SOS) Alerts.
  • 6. Device can be configured to hit multiple servers for a single event.
  • 7. Vehicle health & Behaviour Monitoring (OBD-II).
  • 8. Driver Behaviour Monitoring.
  • 9. Configuration parameters can be updated through SMS.
  • 10. Configuration Parameters protected by Authenticate Password / Master number.
  • 11. Auto APN Setting overridden by manual APN Setting.
  • 12. Supports Multi-Operator (Up to 4 Operator Profiles).
  • 13. Supports Dual- Sim (Plastic SIM + M2M SIM).
  • 14. Reverse Geo-Address from device (optional).
  • 15. 5 Circular Geo-fencing inside device.
  • 16. Generate Alerts for Internal / External Battery low voltage level.
  • 17. Configurable External & Internal Battery Cut-off.
  • 18. Complete auto cut-off from vehicle battery to prevent battery discharge.
  • 19. Automated FOTA (firmware updating via GPRS in moving condition).

Who are WE?
Embedded Designs Services India Pvt. Ltd. is among the 20 most promising electronics designs service provider company in India. Travelling ahead of time, trends and burgeoning technology demand driven by its rich R&D efforts, EDS India caters to every requirement in the electronics product development domain under one roof, starting right from Architecture Design to complete Hardware, Software & Mobile Apps.
We at EDS India provide solutions in field of embedded electronics, power electronics, analog and digital design starting from the paper concept to the working prototypes. With an average industry experience of over 15 years our team is motivated and capable to provide robust solution for our customers and partners. We along with our partners have setups accross India thus can extend our solution at pan India levels, we at EDS India are capable to provide support to our end customers across all geographical locations in India.

Our Mission is to provide robust technology solutions to our valued customers and securing the best position in the eyes of our customers and employees.

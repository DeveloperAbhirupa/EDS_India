Team EDS

Managing Director - Mr. Santanu Roy

With more than 23 years of industry experience in Project Management, Designing for Analog & Digital Circuits, Embedded Controls, Power electronics, Hardware & Software Development, Mr Santanu Roy started his carrier in 1996 working with hard core design companies. Mr. Roy has designed and successfully delivered various embedded hardware and software products / technologies to the global market, holding 3 US patents in his name.

Prior to EDS India, Mr. Santanu worked for semiconductor giants like ST Microelectronics where he handled team of FAE’s across Pan India supporting customers of ST Microelectronics and also worked for World leaders like Pentair Inc where by designing and re-engineering products of Pentair Water solution for India, United States and European markets.

Mr Roy has a B.E (Electronics & Tele-Communication) degree along with PGDBA (Operations) from Symbiosis Pune.

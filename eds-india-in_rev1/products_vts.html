ETrack - Device USP

  • Hard, Compact, Light-weight unit with Environment friendly IP67 or IP65 plastic casing.
  • Smart data acquisition – Optimized based on Event, Time, Angle, Ignition & Distance.
  • GPS Dark zone / multi path elimination using LBS & Cell Tower Triangulation.
  • Virtual trip meter with automatic Trip start and Trip end detection.
  • Power / SIM / Battery & Box Tamper detection & Emergency (SOS) Alerts.
  • Configurable SMS Alerts from device on Tampering, Over speeding or Vehicle Towing.
  • Remote Log maintenance and support using SMS.
  • Device can be configured to hit multiple servers for a single event.
  • Vehicle health & Behaviour Monitoring (OBD-II).
  • Driver Behaviour Monitoring.
  • Configuration parameters can be updated through SMS.
  • Configuration Parameters protected by Authenticate Password / Master number.
  • Configurable SMS Alerts.
  • Auto APN Setting overridden by manual APN Setting.
  • Supports Multi-Operator (Up to 4 Operator Profiles).
  • Supports Dual- Sim (Plastic SIM + M2M SIM).
  • Reverse Geo-Address from device (optional).
  • Events from I/O elements detection and sending via GPRS or SMS.
  • 5 Circular Geo-fencing inside device.
  • Internal / External Battery monitoring.
  • Generate Alerts for Internal / External Battery low voltage level.
  • Configurable External & Internal Battery Cut-off.
  • Complete auto cut-off from vehicle battery to prevent battery discharge.
  • Automated FOTA (firmware updating via GPRS in moving condition).
